About us

We are a community-first agency providing trusted Rohingya interpreting and translation services across health, legal, settlement and government sectors. Interpreters are recruited from the Rohingya community and trained in privacy and professional standards.

How to use

  1. Request service via the contact form or call our phone line.
  2. Tell us date/time, type of appointment and location (phone / in-person).
  3. We confirm and allocate an interpreter with the right dialect & expertise.
